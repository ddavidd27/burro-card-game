How to Play Burro

  • Goal: Be the last player standing by avoiding the word "BURRO".
  • Players: 2 to 10 can play.
  • Cards: Each player starts with 4 cards (e.g., 7 of espadas).
  • Passing: Take turns passing one card to the next player. You can pass the card you just got, unless you have 5 cards.
  • Burro: If someone has 4 cards of the same rank (e.g., four 7s), everyone can click "Shout Burro!".
  • Scoring: The slowest to shout gets a letter (B, U, R, R, O). Spell "BURRO" and you’re out!
  • Winning: The last player without "BURRO" wins!
